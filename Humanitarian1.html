Dr. Frederick Morgan — Trauma Orthopedic Surgeon

Board-certified trauma orthopedic surgeon with more than 15 years in high-stakes operative trauma care, limb salvage, and multi-system reconstruction. Clinical base in Tampa, FL, with permanent international surgical commitments.

Dr. Morgan has led life-saving orthopedic procedures in metropolitan trauma centers, disaster zones, and conflict field hospitals. He combines technical precision with humanitarian commitment — training local surgical teams, implementing trauma systems, and advancing limb salvage techniques.

He is founder of the Bayshore Private Trauma Center and director of the Morgan Global Surgical Fellowship for early-career trauma surgeons.
