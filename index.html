Bayshore Private Hospital – Tampa, Florida

Dr. Frederick Alen Morgan is a board-certified Orthopedic Trauma Surgeon and consultant with the Bayshore Medical Group, providing specialized surgical expertise across several leading healthcare institutions in Tampa. With active consulting privileges at Tampa General Hospital and St. Joseph’s Hospital, Dr. Morgan delivers advanced orthopedic and trauma care within collaborative, hospital-based settings—focusing on precision surgery, evidence-based treatment, and compassionate, patient-centered recovery. Operating exclusively through his hospital affiliations, Dr. Morgan serves patients referred through these premier facilities rather than a single private office location.

As a leading regional center for orthopedic trauma and reconstructive surgery, Bayshore Private Hospital houses advanced trauma suites, hybrid operating theaters, and a 24/7 critical care wing supported by multidisciplinary specialists. The hospital’s Comprehensive Trauma Program is accredited by the American College of Surgeons and is renowned for its rapid-response surgical teams and cutting-edge imaging systems that facilitate precision in complex cases.

Dr. Frederick Alen Morgan serves as a consulting orthopedic trauma surgeon under locum tenens appointments, bringing unparalleled expertise in the management of complex fractures, limb salvage, polytrauma, and revision reconstructive procedures. His presence elevates Bayshore’s trauma division, providing advanced operative techniques and leadership in surgical education.

The hospital also partners with Johns Hopkins All Children’s and University of South Florida Health for collaborative trauma research and continuing education, ensuring the integration of academic insight with clinical excellence.

Beyond its surgical prowess, Bayshore offers a holistic healing environment — including ocean-view recovery suites, private concierge services, physiotherapy and hydrotherapy centers, and personalized wellness programs. Patients and families receive dedicated support from multilingual care teams, embodying the hospital’s philosophy: “Healing the body, uplifting the spirit.”

Bio

Dr. Frederick Alen Morgan is an internationally recognized trauma orthopedic surgeon specializing in restoring mobility and function in the most challenging injuries worldwide. His expertise spans complex fractures, limb salvage, multi-system trauma interventions, and joint reconstruction.

With over 15 years of high-stakes surgical experience, he has led life-saving procedures in conflict zones, disaster areas, and advanced trauma centers. He has trained hundreds of surgeons globally in advanced trauma care, limb reconstruction, and complex revision surgeries — earning a reputation for succeeding where others cannot.

Internationally revered by colleagues across continents, he has served as a visiting consultant and trainer in top medical institutions worldwide, organizing advanced surgical workshops and international master classes attended by elite surgeons seeking to refine their skills.

Known for his original and highly specialized approach to limb preservation and joint replacement, Dr. Morgan has pioneered innovative techniques in minimally invasive orthopedic surgery, cartilage repair, and prosthetic integration. His work has been published in prestigious international journals, becoming a reference point in the field of mobility restoration and patient-centered recovery.

His humanitarian missions with Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF), the United Nations (UN), and the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) reflect a lifelong dedication to providing urgent, high-quality trauma care where it is needed most. He also serves as a global advisor for the World Health Organization (WHO) on disaster trauma readiness.

Beyond the operating room, Dr. Morgan’s passion for humanitarian medicine has taken him across borders — from conflict zones to underserved rural communities. He sees surgery not only as a science but as an art form rooted in restoring dignity through mobility. This same philosophy fuels his deep interest in design, architecture, and contemporary art, which he considers essential sources of inspiration for both his medical and personal life.

A committed traveler without borders, Dr. Morgan balances his professional life with a love for exploration, immersing himself in diverse cultures while sharing his expertise wherever the need is greatest. He currently carries out his clinical activity across multiple international hospitals, maintaining a base of operations in the United States, with close collaborations in Europe, Africa, and Asia.

On the personal front, Dr. Morgan was previously married to Jannie Goodman, with the union concluding in divorce nine years ago; the couple had no offspring. With an estimated estate valued at $7 million, he has accrued his wealth through a succession of high-profile clinical engagements and consultancy roles. Outside the operating theater, he cultivates interests in contemporary art curation, nautical pursuits, and the mentorship of emerging surgical talent.
